Top movies of all-time.

Here are 3 of the best movies from all time, according to various critics and film enthusiasts:

The Godfather (1972)

This classic film from Francis Ford Coppola tells the story of the Corleone family, a powerful Mafia clan in New York City.
It is widely considered to be one of the greatest films ever made, and has been praised for its complex characters, its suspenseful plot, and its masterful direction.

Citizen Kane (1941)

This groundbreaking film from Orson Welles is a fictional biography of Charles Foster Kane, a wealthy newspaper magnate. It is known for its innovative use of cinematography, its nonlinear storytelling, and its exploration of themes such as power, wealth, and ambition.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

This critically acclaimed film from Frank Darabont tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a man who is wrongly convicted of murder and sentenced to life in prison.
It is a story of hope, redemption, and the power of the human spirit.
